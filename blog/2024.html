A celebration of playable arts & culture


Relive that June vibe! An upcoming games showcase!

2024 was a banner year for TGW, with close to 3000 combined attendees (3X our first year) at the 25 events over seven magical days!

It kicked off in the glorious Great Hall on Queen West courtesy of Interactive Ontario and XP Game Summit... (photo: Brian de Rivera Simon)

...and went Out of Bounds with a 12 hour speed-running marathon with 13K streaming viewers in addition to the live audience at See-Scape! (photo: greatnewtons)

The Night Parkcade: Forest Frolick invited brave souls deep into a ravine by a river led only by the clues of a mysterious Scroll of Invitation... (photo: Dean Tomlinson)

And the Pekoe Picnic at the Grange treated people to Tea! (photo: Zelda Bee)

A live performance satirizing precarious work in Internity... (Photo: Iain Soder)

The Toronto Reference Library hosted the Broken Games Collective's Play and Preserve talks and hands-on hardware exhibits. (photo: Victoria Fard)

Kids (and kids-at-heart) were invited to design their Dream Game covers, then get a theme song created on the spot by Hypergame Storytime! (Photo: Mike Wood)

And we closed out the week with a party featuring Hand Eye Society's Torontron Bartops, TOJam games and a '90s video dance party hosted by Mighty Yell! (Photo: Douglas Gregory)
