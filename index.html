Toronto Games Week is a celebration of playable arts and culture! The first Toronto Games Week will be on June 1-7, 2023 and is a collective coordination of events organized independently by dozens of organizations, creators, curators, and companies.

  • Kick off party & social in a sci-fi bar!
  • Pop-up arcade in a park after dark!
  • Talk on Italian cinema influencing survival horror!
  • Comic launch about co-ops and unions in games!
  • Talk on ancient artifacts of Nintendo!
  • Workshop for autobiographical games!
