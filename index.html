Toronto Games Week is a celebration of playable arts and culture! Toronto Games Week 2024 will be on June 13-19 and is a collective coordination of events organized independently by dozens of organizations, creators, curators, and companies.

At Toronto Games Week this year, you might:

  • Frolic in a flickering forest pop-up arcade after dark!
  • Learn open source game tools at a workshop!
  • Play or cheer at a women & femme speedrunning event!
  • Unearth videogame preservation in the Mystery Dump!
  • Sip or stroll the East End Barcade Crawl!
  • Install your game on a Gameboy cartridge!

The week is being kicked off by the XP Summit, a two day industry event that brings together international and local game development talent to learn from each other.

We’re excited to be bringing a few special guests to town that will be appearing in XP and TGW events:

  • Trey Smith, veteran creative director from Vancouver (Hardspace: Shipbreaker, Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 3)
  • Xalavier Nelson Jr., BAFTA nominated narrative designer from El Paso, Texas (Stranger Things VR, Hypnospace Outlaw)
  • Phil Salvador, library director from San Francisco (Video Game History Foundation)

If you’d like to run a TGW event yourself, there’s still time to get in on the fun! Fill out this form. We’re open to including board/escape room/theatrical/LARPing -- playful culture of all sorts, not just screen based games!

If you’d like to volunteer for Toronto Games Week and help the coalition of community organizations and not-for-profits that make it happen, consider signing up here. Even at this early stage there’s lots of interesting things to do!

TGW is happening thanks to the support of Ontario Creates, City of Toronto Creative Tech, https://store.epicgames.com/en-US/, Drinkbox Studio. Please contact us about promotional opportunities.
