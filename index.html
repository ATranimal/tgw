A celebration of playable arts & culture



Toronto Games Week (June 13-19, 2024) is a collective coordination of events organized independently by dozens of organizations, curators, companies, creators, and communities.

At Toronto Games Week this year, you might:

  • Frolic in a flickering forest pop-up arcade after dark!
  • Learn open source game tools at a workshop!
  • Play or cheer at a women & femme speedrunning event!
  • Brew a DIY blend at a tea party in the park!
  • Compare working class sci-fi movies and games!
  • Install a game you made on a Gameboy cartridge!

Special guests

We're kicking off Toronto Games Week 2024 with XP Summit, a two day industry event that brings together international and local game development talent to learn from each other. And we’re excited to be bringing special guests to town:

  • Trey Smith, veteran creative director from Vancouver (Hardspace: Shipbreaker, Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 3)
  • Xalavier Nelson Jr., BAFTA nominated narrative designer from El Paso, Texas (Stranger Things VR, Hypnospace Outlaw)
  • Phil Salvador, library director from San Francisco (Video Game History Foundation)
